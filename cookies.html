Cookie Policy

PollSlide Technologies LLC · Effective date:

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. We also use similar technologies such as local storage and session storage. This policy covers all of these technologies.

What cookies we use

Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are required for the Service to function. They cannot be disabled.

Cookie/Storage Purpose Duration
Firebase Auth token Keeps you signed in to your PollSlide account Session / persistent (local storage)
ql_pid_*, ql_pname_* Remembers a participant's name and session ID so they don't have to re-enter it if they refresh the page Session (sessionStorage)
ql_liveStart_*, ql_theme Stores per-question session start times and your UI theme preference (dark/light) Persistent (localStorage)
ps_cookieConsent Records your cookie consent decision so we don't ask again 1 year

Analytics cookies (optional)

We currently do not use third-party analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics). If we add them in future, this policy will be updated and your consent will be requested before they are set.

Marketing cookies

We do not use any marketing or advertising cookies.

Managing your cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time:

  • Cookie settings banner: Click "Cookie settings" in the website footer to review and update your preferences.
  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking strictly necessary cookies will prevent the Service from functioning correctly. See your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • localStorage / sessionStorage: These can be cleared through your browser's developer tools or privacy settings.

Third-party services

The Service uses Google Firebase, which may set its own cookies for authentication purposes. These are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Changes to this policy

We will update this Cookie Policy if our practices change. Material changes will be communicated via the consent banner on our website.
