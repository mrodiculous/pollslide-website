Mac Companion (any presentation)

A floating window that shows live results over any presentation — Keynote, PowerPoint or a PDF — and auto-detects your poll's QR.
Available — free Mac download

How to set it up

  1. Download it free from pollslide.com/download, open the .dmg, and drag it into Applications. It's signed & notarized by Apple, so it opens with no security warnings.
  2. Open it and allow Screen Recording when macOS asks — it's used only to spot your slide's QR code; nothing is recorded.
  3. Sign in at app.pollslide.com → account menu → Connect Mac App, and type the 6-character code into the companion.
  4. Do a quick dry run (especially with a second display) so the results window shows on the right screen — drag it there once and it stays. Then present from Keynote: the matching poll appears automatically whenever your QR is on screen.

PowerPoint Add-in

Works in PowerPoint on Windows, Mac and the web — insert live polls and results right onto your slides.
Coming soon

How to set it up

  1. Once approved, get it from Microsoft AppSource — or in PowerPoint, Insert → Get Add-ins and search “PollSlide”.
  2. Open the PollSlide pane and sign in / pair with your account.
  3. Pick a poll and insert its QR or live-results onto the slide.
  4. Present as usual — your audience scans and answers come in live.
