Install in under a minute

1

Download & install

Open the downloaded .dmg, then drag PollSlide Companion into your Applications folder. Double-click to open — at the one-time macOS prompt, click Open.

2

Allow Screen Recording

macOS explains why, then takes you straight to the setting. Toggle it on and reopen the app — one time only. It's used solely to spot your QR code.

3

Pair with your account

Click the menu-bar icon, choose Connect Mac App, and enter the 6-character code from PollSlide's account menu.

4

Dry run, then present

Do a quick practice run — especially with a second display — so the window shows where you want. Then present: it pops up automatically on your QR slide.

🖥️

Using a projector or second display? Do a 30-second dry run first.

This is the single most important step for a trouble-free presentation. With more than one screen connected, the results window can open on the wrong display. Before your audience is watching, bring up a QR slide so the window appears — and if it lands on the wrong screen, simply drag it by its top bar onto the display where your slides are showing. It stays there for the rest of your talk. Repeat this whenever your setup changes — a new room, projector, or TV.

🔒

What Screen Recording actually does here

The companion watches your screen only to detect the PollSlide QR code on your slides. Nothing is ever recorded, saved, or sent anywhere. macOS requires this permission for any app that reads the screen — there is no way around it, by design. The moment the app is closed, it stops entirely.

What's in the app

📍

Auto-detect from your slides

Scans your screen at 6fps. Detects the PollSlide QR and switches to the exact matching question.

🪟

Floats over any full-screen app

A draggable, resizable window that sits above Keynote, PowerPoint, Google Slides or a PDF — without touching your slides or animations.

📊

Live results, all display styles

Bar chart, donut, leaderboard, big numbers — switch modes live. Window auto-sizes to fit every response.

🎉

Confetti on reveal

When the answer is revealed — automatically or when you click Reveal now — a burst of confetti fires across the window.

⌨️

Hotkey control

Control + Option + Y shows or hides the window at any time, even over a full-screen presentation.

🔗

One-click pairing

Links to your pollslide.com account with a 6-character code. No passwords stored on the Mac.
