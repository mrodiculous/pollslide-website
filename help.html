Live polls that play right over your slides.

PollSlide turns any presentation into a two-way conversation. Build questions in minutes, drop a QR code on your slide, and your audience answers from their phones — results appear instantly. This guide walks you through everything, step by step.

🗳️ Polls📋 Surveys🏆 Quizzes📚 Study

👋 Welcome — the 3-step idea

Every PollSlide presentation works the same way, whether it's a poll, survey, quiz, or study set.

1 · Build

Create a presentation and add questions — type them yourself or let AI draft them for you.

2 · Stamp your slide

Each question has a QR code. Drop it on your Keynote, PowerPoint, or a PDF.

3 · Go live

Present. Your audience scans the QR, answers, and results animate in real time.

🗳️ Create your first poll

From a blank account to a ready question in under a minute.

  1. Sign in at app.pollslide.com.
  2. Pick a product tab — for this example, 🗳️ Polls.
  3. Click + New Poll. A fresh presentation is created.
  4. Give it a name (e.g. "Team Trivia Night") and save.
  5. You're ready to add questions — see below.
Tip: On the Free plan you can keep 3 presentations. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited polls, surveys, quizzes, and study sets.

➕ Add a question

  1. Click + Add Question.
  2. Type your question in the question box.
  3. Fill in the answer options (A, B, C, D…).
  4. Choose a question type — single choice, multi-select, rating, word cloud, or free text.
  5. Mark the correct answer (for quizzes) by tapping the ✓ next to an option.
In a hurry? Click ✨ Ask Polly and give it a topic or paste your notes — Polly drafts a full set of questions for you to edit.

✨ Draft it with Polly AI

Polly is PollSlide's built-in AI. Give it a topic — or paste your notes, an outline, or a paragraph — and it writes a whole set of questions in seconds. It works for every product: polls, surveys, quizzes and study sets.

  1. Click ✨ Ask Polly on any presentation, or ✨ New with Polly to start a brand-new one from scratch.
  2. Type what you want — e.g. "5 trivia questions about the solar system" — or paste your own notes to turn into questions.
  3. Pick how many questions (1–30) and, for images, a style.
  4. Polly drafts the questions, options, and correct answers. Edit anything before you go live.

🗳️ Any product

Polly matches what you're building — opinion polls, multi-question surveys, graded quizzes with correct answers, or flashcard study sets.

⚡ Fast & private

Polly runs on PollSlide's own servers first for speed, with a cloud fallback. Your prompts are used only to draft your questions.

Fair-use limits: Polly is metered per month by plan (Free includes a monthly allowance; Pro and Team include much more). If you hit the limit, you can still build questions by hand, or upgrade for more. See Plans & billing.

🖼️ Add an image with AI

  1. Open a question and find the image row.
  2. Click ✨ Generate with AI — PollSlide creates an image from your question text.
  3. Pick a style (Illustration, Realistic, 3D, Cartoon, Minimal, Watercolor).
  4. Prefer your own? Click ⬆ Upload or paste an image / GIF / video URL.
  5. Use the S / M / L / ⛶ buttons to size the media on the slide.

⛶ Present

Present mode shows your question full-screen with a QR code your audience scans to answer.

  1. Click ⛶ Present on any question.
  2. Your audience points their phone camera at the QR code — no app needed.
  3. Watch responses roll in live. Switch chart styles anytime.
  4. Use 📋 Copy QR box for slide to paste the QR straight onto your own Keynote / PowerPoint / any deck.

📱 No phone to scan the QR? No problem.

Anyone on a laptop (or any browser) can join without scanning: they go to pollslide.com/join and type the code shown next to your QR. Same session, same live results.

So your audience always has both options, add this line under the QR on your slide:
📱 Scan the QR — or go to pollslide.com/join and enter code ABC123
In the app, the Share / QR window has a “Copy this line” button that fills in your real code for you.

🔗 Share without presenting

Not every project is presented live. Surveys, quizzes and study sets can be sent out and answered at your audience's own pace — no QR on a slide required.

  1. Open your presentation and click 🔗 Share.
  2. Copy the join link (or its QR code) and post it anywhere — a chat, an LMS, a slide, a printout.
  3. Or click ✉️ Email it to send the link straight to a list of recipients.
  4. Prefer a code? Anyone can go to pollslide.com/join and type the 6-character code.

📋 Surveys

Collect feedback over hours or days. Every response flows into the same live results and CSV export.

📚 Study sets

Learners review flashcards on their own with spaced repetition — share the link and they practice anytime.

One link, one tally: whether people scan a QR live, join by code on a laptop, or open an emailed link, they all land in the same session — your results stay in one place.

📊 Post-reveal & results

When you reveal the answer, PollSlide celebrates with leaderboards, podiums, insight chips, and confetti.

  1. In the editor, choose a post-reveal style: Leaderboard, Podium, Scorecard, or an Explainer / Did-you-know card.
  2. Set how long after the first answer to auto-reveal (or reveal manually).
  3. Add optional explainer text shown after the reveal.
  4. Turn on auto-advance to flow to the next question hands-free.

🎉 Reveal live

  1. Click Reveal now (or let the timer auto-reveal).
  2. The correct answer highlights in green; results lock.
  3. The leaderboard / podium appears with names and response times.
  4. Advance to the next question and keep the momentum going.

📊 Results, reports & export

Every response is saved. Review results live during your talk, or come back to them any time afterward — and take the raw data with you.

  1. During a session, watch results update live — bars, donuts, word clouds, leaderboards and podiums.
  2. Open 📊 Report on any presentation for the full Response Report: totals, per-question breakdowns, and per-participant answers.
  3. Click ⬇ CSV to export everything to a spreadsheet — great for grading, analysis, or your records.
  4. Running the same quiz more than once? Every reset is saved with its date, so past sessions never disappear.

🖥️ Big screen

Click 🖥️ Big screen to open a clean, full-screen results view for a projector or second monitor — no companion app needed.

🔒 Your data

Exports are yours to keep. Delete a session or your whole account any time — see Your data & privacy.

✨ AI Insights — read the room

When your audience answers an open-text question, Polly can read every response and show you the themes, the mood, and a one-line summary — instantly, on your results panel. Perfect for "What's on your mind?", "One word for today", or any free-form question.

  1. Run any open text or word cloud question and let answers roll in — you need at least 2.
  2. On your live results panel, click ✨ AI Insights.
  3. Polly returns a summary, an overall sentiment bar (positive / neutral / negative), and the top themes — each with a count and a real example quote.
  4. Click it again any time to re-analyze as more answers arrive.

🧠 Themes, not noise

Hundreds of replies become a ranked list of what people actually said — grouped, counted, with an example each.

🔒 Private & included

Polly analyzes on PollSlide's own servers first — not shipped to a third-party AI cloud — and it's included in your plan.

Tip: AI Insights shines on open-ended questions. For multiple-choice, your live bars and the Report already show the full breakdown.

🖥️ The Mac Companion

PollSlide Companion is a free macOS app that floats your live results over any presentation — it even auto-detects the on-screen QR code and shows the matching poll. Download it free →

🔗 Set up the Mac Companion — step by step

Follow these seven steps once and you're set for every presentation. It takes about two minutes. Requirements: a Mac on macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later — it's free.

1 Download it

Go to pollslide.com/download and click Download for Mac. A file called PollSlideCompanion.dmg lands in your Downloads. It's signed & notarized by Apple.

2 Open the disk image

In your Downloads, double-click PollSlideCompanion.dmg. A little install window opens.

3 Drag it into Applications

In the window that opens, drag the PollSlide Companion icon onto the Applications folder beside it.

⚠ Don't skip this
Running it straight from the disk image or Downloads stops macOS from remembering the Screen-Recording permission — so auto-detection won't work. It must live in Applications.

4 Open it the first time

Open your Applications folder and double-click PollSlide Companion. macOS asks once whether you're sure — click Open. A small chart icon 📊 appears in your menu bar (top-right).

5 Allow Screen Recording

The app asks for Screen Recording — this is how it spots the QR code on your slide. It opens Settings for you: turn the switch ON for PollSlide Companion, then Quit & Reopen the app when macOS offers.

⚠ Needed for auto-detect
It's used only to read your PollSlide QR — nothing is recorded, saved, or sent anywhere.

6 Pair with your account

On pollslide.com, open your account menu → Connect Mac App to get a 6-character code. Click the menu-bar chart icon 📊 → Connect Mac App, and type that code in. Done — you're linked.

7 Dry run, then present

Present your slides as usual — the results window pops up on its own whenever your QR slide is on screen, and hides when you move on. Toggle it anytime with Control-Option-Y.

Using a projector or second screen? Do a quick dry run first: if the window opens on the wrong display, drag it by its top bar onto the screen your slides are on — it stays there for the whole talk.
Still not popping up on your QR slide? 99% of the time it's one of two things: (1) the app isn't in your Applications folder — move it there (Step 3); or (2) Screen Recording is off, or the app needs a restart after you turned it on — check Step 5, then quit the app (menu-bar icon → Quit) and reopen it. Make sure you're on version 1.3.3 or later.

🛠️ Troubleshooting

The quick fixes for the handful of things that trip people up.

The companion isn't popping up on my QR slide

99% of the time it's one of two things: (1) the app isn't in your Applications folder — move it there; or (2) Screen Recording is off, or the app needs a restart after you enabled it. Turn it on in System Settings, then quit the app from its menu-bar icon and reopen it. Make sure you're on the latest version from pollslide.com/download.

My audience can't join or results aren't updating

PollSlide needs an internet connection on both your device and your audience's. Check you're online, then have them re-scan the QR or go to pollslide.com/join and enter the code. Refresh the presenter page if a session looks stuck.

The QR code won't scan

Make it bigger on your slide and keep good contrast (dark on light). Most phone cameras scan automatically — if not, any QR app works, or use the pollslide.com/join + code option instead.

Polly AI is slow or didn't generate

Try again — the first request after a while can be slower. If you've hit your monthly Polly allowance, you'll be told; you can still add questions by hand or upgrade for more. See Plans & billing.

The results window opened on the wrong screen

Drag it by its top bar onto the display your slides are on — it stays there for the rest of your talk. With a projector or second display, always do a quick dry run first so you can position it once.

Still stuck?

Email help@pollslide.com and check the status page to see if everything's running normally.

❓ FAQ

Does my audience need to install anything?

No. They just scan the QR code with their phone camera and answer in the browser.

Can I use PollSlide with Keynote, PowerPoint or a PDF?

Yes — any of them. Use Copy QR box for slide to paste the QR onto any slide, or run the Mac Companion to float live results over whatever's on your screen — Keynote, PowerPoint, a PDF, even a webpage. There's also a PowerPoint add-in.

How do I show results on the projector?

Use Present mode in the browser, or run the Mac Companion app for a floating results window that stays on top of your slides.

What's the difference between Reveal and Post-reveal?

Reveal locks responses and highlights the correct answer. Post-reveal is what shows next — a leaderboard, podium, scorecard, or explainer card.

Why does the companion ask for Screen Recording?

Only to read the QR code on your current slide so it shows the right poll automatically. Nothing is captured or sent.

Is there a free plan?

Yes. The Free plan lets you build and run real polls, surveys, quizzes and study sets, with a monthly Polly AI allowance — no credit card required. Upgrade only when you need unlimited presentations, larger audiences, or more Polly. See Plans & billing.

How many people can join a poll?

It depends on your plan — the Free plan is capped for smaller rooms, and paid plans raise the limit substantially. The pricing page shows the current participant caps for each plan.

Do I need to download anything?

No download is required to build or present — everything runs in your browser at app.pollslide.com. The Mac Companion is an optional free app that floats live results over your slides; your audience never installs anything either.

Can I use PollSlide in another language?

Yes. The interface and this site auto-detect your browser language (English, Spanish, German, French, Portuguese and Italian), and your audience's answer screen is translated too. You can also switch language manually.

Can I present online over Zoom, Meet or Teams?

Absolutely. Share your screen as usual and present from the PollSlide app, or run the Mac Companion so live results float over whatever you're screen-sharing. Remote audiences scan the on-screen QR or join at pollslide.com/join.

Who owns the data and responses?

You do. Export everything to CSV any time, and delete a session or your entire account whenever you like. See Your data & privacy.

How do I cancel, and is there a refund?

Manage or cancel any time from account menu → Billing. Every paid plan is covered by a 14-day money-back guarantee — just email help@pollslide.com.

Is my payment secure?

Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe; PollSlide never sees or stores your card number.
